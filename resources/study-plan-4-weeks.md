## Tips for Success

1. **Consistency is Key**
   - Study daily, even if just 1 hour
   - Better than cramming

2. **Active Learning**
   - Don't just read - code along
   - Explain concepts out loud
   - Teach someone else

3. **Spaced Repetition**
   - Review flashcards daily
   - Revisit weak topics multiple times

4. **Mock Interviews**
   - Practice out loud
   - Record yourself
   - Get feedback

5. **Stay Healthy**
   - Good sleep (7-8 hours)
   - Regular breaks
   - Exercise

6. **Track Progress**
   - Mark completed sections
   - Celebrate small wins
   - Adjust plan as needed

## Adjustment for Different Timelines

### 2-Week Intensive (4-5 hours/day)
- Week 1: JavaScript, TypeScript, React (Days 1-7)
- Week 2: System Design, Practice, Mocks (Days 8-14)
- Skip: Deep dives, build only 1 project
- Focus: Most common interview questions

### 8-Week Relaxed (1-1.5 hours/day)
- Weeks 1-2: JavaScript & TypeScript
- Weeks 3-4: React & Next.js
- Weeks 5-6: System Design & Advanced Topics
- Weeks 7-8: Practice & Mock Interviews
- Build: 3-4 projects
- Deep understanding of all topics
