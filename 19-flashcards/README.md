## üéì Study Strategies

### 1. Active Recall
- Read question
- Cover answer
- Try to answer out loud
- Check if correct
- If wrong, understand why

### 2. Feynman Technique
- After answering, explain concept as if teaching a beginner
- If you can't explain simply, you don't understand it well

### 3. Interleaving
- Don't do all cards from one topic
- Mix topics for better retention
- Use curated decks that mix topics

### 4. Testing Effect
- Quiz yourself regularly
- Don't just read - actively recall
- Makes retrieval stronger

## üí° Tips for Success

1. **Consistency > Intensity**
   - 15 min daily better than 2 hours once a week

2. **Focus on Understanding**
   - Don't memorize verbatim
   - Understand concepts deeply

3. **Use Multiple Senses**
   - Read (visual)
   - Say out loud (auditory)
   - Write down (kinesthetic)

4. **Teach Someone**
   - Best way to solidify knowledge
   - Explain cards to a friend or rubber duck

5. **Track Your Progress**
   - Mark cards you struggle with
   - Celebrate when you master difficult cards
